# Badger of the Mystic Forest — Unlock Reference

A complete list of every **character** and every **trophy**, and exactly how
each one is earned. This is the single source of truth for unlock conditions
(mirrors `src/Achievements.js` and the awarding logic in `src/Game.js`).

- **In-run** feats reset every run; **all-time** feats accumulate across every
  run and persist in your browser's local storage.
- Anything you haven't unlocked yet shows its how-to as `???` in the in-game
  Achievements viewer — this file is the spoiler-filled reference.

## Notes on a few tricky ones

- **Hastings Is A Place On Earth** (exactly 106.6) is deliberately a
  near-impossible "white whale" — the game's point values don't combine
  cleanly to a `.6` ending, so treat it as a curiosity.
- **The three-station trophies** (*End of the Line*, *Billy Rocketfingers*)
  mean the three **original** stops (Upper Cottage Lane, Docklands, Mystic
  Forest Central). **Cactus Junction** is a bonus secret stop and does not
  count toward them.
- **Cactus Junction** only appears as an option on the ticket machine
  itself; its roundel sign appears in the desert once you've ridden there.
- The **whirlpool** pays a random −45.45…+45.45 once per dip — the easiest
  route to *Negative Nelly* (finish in the red) is a run of bad spins.
